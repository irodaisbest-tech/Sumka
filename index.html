Zamona D'Oro

ZAMONA embodies the vibrant Eastern spirit, deeply rooted in the rich textile heritage of Uzbekistan. D'ORO represents the pinnacle of Italian luxury, where artisanal mastery meets gold-standard perfection.

Our mission is to present Uzbekistan to the world through high-end fashion. Every piece is an emotional journey across the ancient Silk Road, combining vibrant Uzbek suzani patterns with minimalist Milanese silhouettes.
